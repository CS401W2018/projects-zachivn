Final Project Proposal

Title and Topic

Title: Beautiful Places to Visit in Puerto Princesa, Palawan

Topic: A travel guide to the most stunning destinations in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, Philippines, showcasing its natural beauty, cultural landmarks, and must-visit attractions.

Why I Chose This Topic

Puerto Princesa, often called the "City in a Forest," boasts some of the most stunning natural wonders on the planet. I picked this topic because I want to share its beauty with others and encourage more people to discover this tropical paradise.

Content Overview

Home Page: - Introduction to Puerto Princesa
- Featured image of a popular destination
- Navigation to the main pages
- A welcome message highlighting the purpose of the site

Page 1: Natural Wonders - Highlights of attractions like the Underground River and Honda Bay
- You'll find photos and descriptions of each place
- Handy tips for visitors

Page 2: Cultural Landmarks - Discover places like the Palawan Heritage Center
- Learn about local festivals and traditions
- Cool images and some historical background

Page 3: Travel Tips - A table with suggested itineraries (like where to go and how much time to spend)
- Transportation tips
- Advice on where to stay and how to budget

Page 4: Interactive Feedback - A form where visitors can share their experiences
- A gallery of photos from other travelers
- Travel tips from fellow visitors

Page 5: References and Resources - List of references
- External links to travel blogs, local government websites, and guides
- Credits for all images and content
