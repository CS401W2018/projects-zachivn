Plan Your Visit

Here are some tips for a smooth and enjoyable trip:

Sample Itinerary
Day Destination Activity Estimated Cost
Day 1 Arrival and City Tour Explore the Palawan Heritage Center to immerse yourself in Palawan’s culture. $40
Day 2 Underground River Guided tour $50
Day 3 Honda Bay Island hopping $30
Day 4 Underground River Adventure Take a guided tour of the Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. $200
Day 5 Iwahig River Firefly Watching Spend the evening on a magical firefly-watching cruise along the Iwahig River. $100
Day 6 Mitra’s Ranch and Souvenir Shopping Visit Mitra’s Ranch for panoramic views of the city. Shop for local crafts and delicacies at the Pasalubong Center or souvenir shops downtown $100-200
Day 7 Nagtabon Beach Escape Unwind at Nagtabon Beach, a peaceful spot perfect for swimming and surfing. $60
